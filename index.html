About Me

I am a postdoc at the University of Southern California in the Department of Preventive Medicine studying the associations between environmental and genetic and cancer.

Research

My research focuses on utilizing geospatially derived environmental exposures to assess their relationship with health outcomes: primarily in hematological malignancies. A list of publications can be found at my ORCID profile.
